Welcome Message

Dear Friends and Colleagues, it is our immense pleasure to invite Indonesian Ophthalmologist to the REVIEWS I that will be held from December 4th to 6th, 2020. REVIEWS I was originally scheduled to be held in Makassar and later we decided to organize the congress entirely with a web-based congress to be presented via virtual streaming in light of the rapid increase in the number of COVID-19 positive case "or" spreading Covid-19 pandemic in the world "or" due to continuing concern related to the COVID-19 pandemic. There will be Plenary lecture, Symposium, Didactic Course, and Exhibiton, which will allow each of us to learn the latest information and refresh our knowledge in ophthalmology. This seminar will be held by support from Center Comitte of The Indonesian Ophthalmologists Association (IOA) and initation by collaboration between fifth branches of IOA : West Nusa Tenggara, South Sulawesi, North Sulawesi and Bali as a part of Regional V of IOA.

Andi Muhammad Ichsan, MD

Chairman of Indonesian Ophthalmologist Association South Sulawesi Branch
